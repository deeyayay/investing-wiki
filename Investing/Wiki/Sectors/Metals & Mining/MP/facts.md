## Company Overview

MP Materials is the only company in the United States mining, processing, and manufacturing rare earth permanent magnets — the complete rare earth supply chain from mine to magnet. Mountain Pass (California) is the only operating rare earth mine in North America; the Fort Worth magnet manufacturing facility (Stage III) produces NdFeB (neodymium-iron-boron) permanent magnets for GM electric vehicle motors under a 20-year supply agreement. China controls ~80% of global rare earth processing and has begun restricting rare earth magnet exports — making MP Materials the only commercially viable domestic alternative for US defense and automotive applications.
