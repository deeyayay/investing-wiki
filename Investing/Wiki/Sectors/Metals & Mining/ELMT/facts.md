## Company Overview

The Elmet Group is the leading US manufacturer of tungsten and molybdenum specialty products — refractory metals that maintain strength at extreme temperatures and are critical for defense, semiconductor manufacturing, and aerospace. Elmet's core products include tungsten carbide components (cutting tools, armor-piercing ammunition), molybdenum electrodes (used in glass fiber and LCD manufacturing), and precision tungsten parts for defense applications (kinetic energy penetrators, X-ray targets). In semiconductor manufacturing, tungsten chemical vapor deposition (CVD) fills the metal interconnects that wire together billions of transistors in each chip. China controls ~85% of global tungsten supply and has imposed export controls — making Elmet a strategic domestic alternative for US defense and chip manufacturing.
