## Company Overview

Freeport-McMoRan is the world's largest publicly traded copper producer — operating Grasberg (Indonesia), Morenci (Arizona, the largest US copper mine), and Cerro Verde (Peru). Copper is the critical metal for electrification: every EV requires 80-100kg of copper (vs. 20kg for ICE); every MW of offshore wind requires 8,000kg; AI data center power delivery requires vast copper wiring. The structural supply deficit is well-documented — greenfield copper projects take 15-20 years from discovery to production. Freeport's new atmospheric leaching technology unlocks previously uneconomic oxide copper deposits, potentially adding 2B+ lbs of annual production from existing mine footprints without new greenfield capex.
