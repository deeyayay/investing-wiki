## Company Overview

MercadoLibre is the dominant e-commerce and fintech compounder in Latin America — the closest thing to an Amazon + PayPal combination across a region of 650M+ people with massive under-penetration in both e-commerce and formal banking. Mercado Libre (marketplace) and Mercado Pago (fintech/payments) reinforce each other via a four-flywheel structure: payments fund logistics → logistics drives GMV → GMV grows the lending book → lending profits fund further investment. Co-founder Marcos Galperin held the CEO role for 26 years (1999–2025) and remains Executive Chairman with ~7% ownership.
