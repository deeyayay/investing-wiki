## Company Overview

Robinhood Markets pioneered commission-free retail trading and has evolved into a multi-product financial platform: equities/options trading, crypto exchange (enhanced by Bitstamp acquisition 2025), Gold premium subscription ($10/month), cash management, and international expansion (UK, EU). Co-founder Vlad Tenev holds ~8% equity and super-voting control. Revenue is highly sensitive to crypto market conditions and trading volumes, creating cyclicality. Gold subscription is the most defensible recurring revenue stream.
