# ASTS — AST SpaceMobile
*Last updated: May 7, 2026*

---

## One-Line Thesis
Building the world's first space-based cellular broadband network capable of connecting standard smartphones directly to satellites — no special hardware required.

---

## Investment Thesis
AST SpaceMobile is attempting something no one has done at scale: replacing cell towers with low-earth orbit satellites to deliver 5G directly to unmodified smartphones. The addressable market is every person on earth with a mobile phone — particularly the 3B+ in areas with poor or no terrestrial coverage. The company has secured partnerships with AT&T, Verizon, Rakuten, and Vodafone, which both validates the technology and provides a distribution moat. BlueBird satellites are now in orbit and commercially operational. The thesis is that if AST can prove consistent, scalable coverage at acceptable economics, it becomes critical infrastructure for the major carriers — and potentially for governments and enterprise customers who need resilient connectivity.

**Key moat:** First-mover in direct-to-device broadband from space, carrier partnerships, massive patent portfolio, proprietary large-aperture satellite antenna design.

**Key risks:** Execution risk on constellation buildout, capital intensity, spectrum licensing complexity, SpaceX Starlink competitive pressure.

**Execution track record:** Avellan has a credible prior exit (EMC, $550M). At ASTS, the team successfully launched and commercially activated the first-generation BlueBird satellites and secured $1.2B in contracted partner commitments plus $3.9B+ in pro-forma liquidity. However, the company has missed prior launch timeline targets, and Block 2 satellites (3x larger, incorporating unproven ASIC chips) have not yet launched, representing the primary execution risk ahead.

**Insider ownership / alignment:** Abel Avellan and permitted transferees control ~71.6% of voting power, qualifying ASTS as a Nasdaq "controlled company." Key carrier partners (AT&T, Vodafone, American Tower, Rakuten) are party to a Stockholders' Agreement, further aligning strategic interests. Avellan's economic ownership exceeds 20% of total equity. His zero cash compensation structure and milestone-vesting equity create strong alignment — he bears real cost when timelines slip. (Source: DEF 14A filed 2025-10-28.)
