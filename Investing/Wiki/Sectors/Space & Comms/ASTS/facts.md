## Company Overview

AST SpaceMobile is building the first and only space-based cellular broadband network capable of connecting directly to standard smartphones — no terminals, no modifications. The BlueBird satellites use massive phased-array antennas (hundreds of square meters of aperture) to amplify mobile signals from low Earth orbit, communicating with the same cellular frequencies already licensed to mobile network operators. MNOs (AT&T, Verizon, Vodafone, Rakuten) pay ASTS a wholesale fee to provide satellite coverage on their existing subscriber plans. The addressable market is every MNO subscriber in areas with poor coverage — 3.5B+ people globally.

- Market cap: ~$10B
