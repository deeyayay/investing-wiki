## Company Overview

Navitas Semiconductor pioneered GaNFast power ICs — the first to monolithically integrate a GaN transistor with gate drive and protection in a single chip. This removes the board-level components that conventional GaN requires, dramatically reducing switching losses and enabling 3x efficiency versus silicon MOSFET solutions. The key applications are EV on-board chargers, data center power supply units (PSUs), and consumer/industrial charging adapters. The company has also moved into SiC modules (via acquisition of GeneSiC 2022) for higher-power EV inverter applications where GaN's voltage ceiling is insufficient.
