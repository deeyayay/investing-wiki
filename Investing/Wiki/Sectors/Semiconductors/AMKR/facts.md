## Company Overview

Amkor Technology is the world's #2 OSAT (Outsourced Semiconductor Assembly and Test) company, with 83% of revenue from advanced packaging — the most technically complex and value-added part of the semiconductor supply chain. As AI chips push the limits of monolithic integration, advanced packaging (2.5D interposers, FOWLP, chiplet assembly) is the engineering bridge. Amkor is one of only three or four companies globally capable of executing this at commercial scale, alongside TSMC's captive CoWoS. Kim family controls 49.4% — five decades of founder alignment.
