## Company Overview

Infineon Technologies is the world's largest automotive semiconductor company and the #1 SiC power semiconductor manufacturer by revenue. The company's CoolSiC MOSFETs are the standard for 800V EV traction inverters — embedded across 40+ OEM vehicle platforms from BMW, Mercedes, Hyundai, and Chinese automakers. The Villach, Austria mega-fab produces 200mm SiC wafers at scale, a year ahead of US competitors. Beyond automotive, Infineon is expanding in data center power conversion (AI servers require 48V power delivery and high-efficiency PSUs) and renewable energy (wind/solar inverters). The security chip division (embedded secure elements for IoT, payment cards, ePassports) provides €3B+ in stable, high-margin recurring revenue.
