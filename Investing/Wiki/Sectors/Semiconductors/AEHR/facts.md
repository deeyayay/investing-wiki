## Company Overview

Aehr Test Systems makes specialized test and burn-in systems for silicon carbide (SiC) wafers — a critical step in EV power module manufacturing. SiC is the material of choice for EV inverters, and every major EV maker needs to test SiC wafers before packaging. Aehr is the only company with a production-ready wafer-level burn-in solution (WaferPak technology), giving it a monopoly-like position in a fast-growing niche. Currently in reset phase after EV cycle softness.

## Key Metrics

- Market cap: ~$1B (small cap; high-volatility)
- Pre-profitability during reset phase
- WaferPak technology: sole production-grade wafer-level SiC burn-in
- Customer concentration: small number of Tier 1 EV suppliers
- Expansion potential: AI chips, photonics test (not yet realized)
- Key watch: quarterly bookings as primary leading indicator
