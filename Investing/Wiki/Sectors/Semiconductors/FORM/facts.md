## Company Overview

FormFactor designs and manufactures wafer probe cards — the interfaces between automatic test equipment and the semiconductor wafer under test. Dominant installed base in DRAM, NAND, logic, and RF probe. In 2025, FormFactor extended its probe expertise into silicon photonics (SiPh/CPO) via the V93000-Triton partnership with Advantest (April 2025) and the Keystone Photonics acquisition (December 2025). The V93000-Triton is the only production-grade opto-electrical CPO test cell — positioned as the chokepoint through which every CPO wafer must pass before packaging.
