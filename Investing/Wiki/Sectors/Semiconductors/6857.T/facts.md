## Company Overview

Advantest is the world's leading semiconductor test equipment company for memory and AI chips. Every HBM4 module destined for an NVIDIA GPU goes through Advantest test systems at SK Hynix and Samsung — the quality gate that ensures NVIDIA can deliver working AI accelerators. Advantest's T5503 and T5577 memory ATE platforms dominate HBM test, and the company is the primary beneficiary of the AI chip quality assurance boom: as GPU clusters require HBM with increasingly stringent reliability specs, test intensity (and Advantest revenue per chip) increases. The V93000 SoC platform also tests AI accelerator logic, and the V93000-Triton joint configuration with FormFactor is the only production-grade co-packaged optics (CPO) test cell. Advantest benefits from every layer of the AI chip stack — memory test, logic test, and emerging CPO test.
