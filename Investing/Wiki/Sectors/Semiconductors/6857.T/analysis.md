## One-Line Thesis

Advantest is the quality gate through which every HBM chip destined for an NVIDIA GPU must pass — as AI chip complexity grows, test intensity grows with it, making Advantest a compounding royalty on the AI semiconductor supply chain.

## Investment Thesis

Every HBM4 memory module that SK Hynix ships to NVIDIA for the Vera Rubin GPU must first pass through Advantest T5503 test systems. There is no shortcut: if a chip passes the Advantest ATE qualification, NVIDIA accepts it. If it doesn't, it's scrap. As HBM becomes the most performance-critical and highest-value component in the AI chip stack, the importance of reliable test — and the willingness to pay for it — has grown substantially.

**The compounding test intensity thesis:** Each generation of AI chip requires more test. HBM4 requires more test vectors than HBM3e, which required more than HBM3. Each increase in test complexity translates to longer test time and higher revenue per chip tested. This is the ATE industry's analog to the ASML "more litho passes per node" dynamic: as chips advance, test revenue per chip increases even at flat unit volumes.

**V93000 + Triton = CPO test monopoly:** The V93000 SoC ATE platform, when combined with FormFactor's Triton optical probe card in the V93000-Triton configuration, creates the only production-grade opto-electrical test cell for co-packaged optics (CPO). This positions Advantest at the test chokepoint for CPO wafers — the next generation of AI chip optical interconnect.

**The AI custom silicon expansion:** Every hyperscaler custom AI chip (Google TPU, AWS Trainium, Microsoft Maia) is tested on SoC ATE — primarily V93000. As hyperscaler custom silicon grows as a percentage of AI compute, Advantest gains alongside TSMC.

**Why 8.0 Unrivaled:**
- HBM test = direct, structural AI chip quality assurance position
- V93000-Triton CPO test monopoly = emerging next-gen position
- ~28-30% operating margin = ATE pricing power validated
- Test intensity grows with chip complexity — revenue per chip increases automatically
- Market cap ~$33B reflects this quality, but the AI chip supercycle is multi-year
