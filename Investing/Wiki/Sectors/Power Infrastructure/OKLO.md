# OKLO — Oklo Inc.
*Last updated: May 7, 2026*

---

## One-Line Thesis
Next-generation nuclear microreactor company positioned to power AI data centers and remote industrial sites; founder-led with a long regulatory runway and no near-term competition at scale.

---

## Investment Thesis
Oklo is building compact fission reactors (Aurora) that can be deployed modularly at scale. The core thesis is that AI data center power demand is growing faster than the grid can handle, and Oklo offers a clean, always-on baseload solution that solar/wind cannot replicate. The company is founder-led (Sam Altman as chairman adds narrative gravity), has DOE backing, and is operating in a category with massive barriers to entry. This is a 5–10 year compounder — the bet is on regulatory approval milestones and first commercial deployments de-risking the thesis over time.

**Key moat:** NRC licensing progress, proprietary fast reactor design, fuel recycling IP, DOE relationships.

**Key risks:** Pre-revenue with long regulatory timeline, NRC COLA approval still pending, capital intensity of nuclear development, execution risk on first commercial deployment.
