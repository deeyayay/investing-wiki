# LWLG — Lightwave Logic
*Last updated: May 11, 2026*

---

## One-Line Thesis
Next-generation electro-optic polymer modulator technology potentially faster and more power-efficient than lithium-niobate or InP alternatives — four Stage 3 design-win customers, Tower Semiconductor partnership, and a 2027 production ramp as the binary catalyst.

## Investment Thesis
Lightwave Logic develops proprietary electro-optic (EO) polymers branded "Perkinamine" enabling optical modulators targeting 200G and 400G-per-lane speeds for AI cluster interconnects. LWLG's polymer-based approach promises to exceed the speed and power limits of current lithium niobate/InP modulator technology while integrating directly onto silicon photonics platforms. As of early 2026: four Stage 3 design-win customers, Tower Semiconductor manufacturing partnership, ~$70M cash runway. The 2027 production ramp is the binary event — a production commitment from any of the four customers re-rates the stock significantly. Pre-revenue, high risk, high reward.

**Key moat:** Proprietary Perkinamine polymer chemistry (deep patent portfolio), Tower Semiconductor partnership provides credible manufacturing path, first-mover in EO polymer on silicon photonics.

**Key risks:** Pre-revenue (binary commercialization outcome), polymer reliability/longevity questions vs. inorganic alternatives, 2027 ramp could slip, $70M cash runway is limited, silicon photonics players could develop competing solutions.
