## Company Overview

Ciena is the #2 global DWDM (Dense Wavelength Division Multiplexing) optical networking system provider, competing with Nokia for the backbone of the internet. The core product is WaveLogic — a proprietary coherent optical DSP ASIC that Ciena designs in-house, enabling higher spectral efficiency than competitors. Networks built on Ciena's WaveLogic platform can carry more data over the same fiber, reducing the need to lay new fiber. Ciena's customer mix spans global telecom carriers (AT&T, BT, Telstra) and web-scale cloud operators (Google, Microsoft, Amazon), the latter representing the fastest-growing and highest-margin segment. The 800ZR coherent pluggable transceiver opens a new data center interconnect opportunity as hyperscalers extend coherent optics inside their campus networks.
