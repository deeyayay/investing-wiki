## One-Line Thesis
Defense + AI laser crossover with dual catalyst structure — a new 70kW-class directed energy milestone drove +92% YTD alongside the NVIDIA photonics wave, making nLIGHT the most differentiated name in the folder by risk profile.

## Investment Thesis
nLIGHT makes high-power fiber lasers and semiconductor lasers for industrial, defense, and microfabrication markets. The dual-catalyst thesis is what makes LASR interesting: defense business is non-correlated to the AI transceiver/interconnect theme, providing a separate revenue driver. On defense: new 70kW-class directed energy laser system announced early 2026 — significant milestone in the DoD's accelerating directed energy weapons (DEW) program. On AI: high-power lasers are used in manufacturing photonic components (dicing, welding, ablation), giving indirect exposure to the photonics supply chain buildout.

**Key moat:** High-power fiber laser expertise, vertically integrated semiconductor laser chip manufacturing, defense customer relationships and security clearances, dual-market buffer.

**Key risks:** Defense contract timing can be lumpy and classified, industrial laser market is competitive (IPG Photonics is dominant), AI exposure is more indirect vs. COHR/LITE/FN, directed energy narrative may already be priced in.
