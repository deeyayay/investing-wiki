## One-Line Thesis
Early-stage CPO pure play crossing the critical inflection from development to commercial execution — first production order received in 2026 (>$5M), 30,000+ optical engines targeted for shipment this year, and ~$430M cash runway.

## Investment Thesis
POET Technologies is building the POET Infinity optical engine platform — an integrated optical engine combining lasers, detectors, and waveguides on a single substrate using their proprietary Optical Interposer technology. Target market is co-packaged optics (CPO) for 400G to 800G+ AI data center interconnects. The company crossed the commercialization threshold in 2026: first production order >$5M, 30,000+ engines projected to ship this year, +101% YTD through April 22. ~$430M cash runway (following multiple financing rounds) provides manufacturing headroom. Manufacturing based in Malaysia.

**Key moat:** Proprietary Optical Interposer technology for monolithic III-V/silicon integration, Malaysia manufacturing cost advantage, $430M cash de-risks near-term execution.

**Key risks:** Very early revenue stage, execution risk on scaling from 30K to high-volume manufacturing, competition from Broadcom/Marvell/Intel, technology leapfrog risk.
