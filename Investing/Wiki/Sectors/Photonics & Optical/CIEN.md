## One-Line Thesis
The coherent optical networking systems backbone — +113% YTD, 3.2T prototype demonstrated at OFC 2026 alongside COHR, and the only large-cap pure-play on optical networking systems (not just components).

## Investment Thesis
Ciena makes the WaveLogic coherent optical networking platforms that underpin long-haul, metro fiber, and hyperscale data center interconnect (DCI) markets. Where COHR and LITE sell components, Ciena sells complete optical networking systems. The AI buildout drives structural demand: hyperscalers are upgrading existing routes from 400G to 800G and 1.6T. Ciena's WaveLogic 6 Extreme (WL6e) chipset supports 1.2Tbps per wavelength; they demonstrated 3.2T technology at OFC 2026 with Coherent. At 49x earnings, mid-range valuation in the sector.

**Key moat:** WaveLogic proprietary DSP chipset (multi-generation performance lead), carrier-grade software, deep telco/hyperscaler relationships, 3.2T roadmap.

**Key risks:** Telco spending cycles, hyperscaler build-vs-buy decisions, competitive pressure from Nokia and Infinera on price.
