# FN — Fabrinet
*Last updated: May 11, 2026*

---

## One-Line Thesis
The contract manufacturer behind the entire optical transceiver supply chain — as 800G/1.6T shipments scale from 24M to 63M+ units in 2026, Fabrinet's order book is a direct read-through on the health of the whole sector.

## Investment Thesis
Fabrinet is the de facto precision optical manufacturing partner for Lumentum, Coherent, and most of the major transceiver makers. They assemble and test finished optical modules at high volume and high precision. As optical transceiver unit volumes surge — projected to jump from ~24M 800G-and-above units in 2025 to ~63M in 2026 — Fabrinet is one of the few companies structurally guaranteed to grow revenue in proportion to that ramp, regardless of which transceiver maker wins market share. Think of it as a royalty on the entire optical supply chain.

**Key moat:** Deep customer lock-in (process transfer switching costs), precision manufacturing expertise in optical alignment, Thailand-based low-cost manufacturing with US-quality standards.

**Key risks:** Concentrated customer base (LITE and COHR are a large % of revenue), any slowdown in hyperscaler optical capex flows through directly, margin pressure as volumes scale.
