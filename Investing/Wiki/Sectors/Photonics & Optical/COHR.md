# COHR — Coherent Corp
*Last updated: May 11, 2026*

---

## One-Line Thesis
The volume leader in optical transceivers with NVIDIA as anchor customer and ~25% market share — the industrial-scale AI photonics compounder with the best risk/reward in the sector.

## Investment Thesis

> **Thesis established:** 2026-05-11
> **Last validated:** 2026-05-12
> **Drift status:** On track — gross margin expanding for 3 consecutive quarters; NVIDIA $2B equity investment confirms strategic supplier status

Coherent is the broadest pure-play in AI optical infrastructure. They supply continuous wave (CW) lasers and transceivers to NVIDIA for the Spectrum-X platform and were named a strategic partner in the Rubin AI rollout. NVIDIA committed $2 billion to Coherent in March 2026 alongside a multi-year purchase commitment — an architectural partnership, not just a supply contract. Coherent gained ~25% market share in optical transceivers by mid-2025 and is shipping at scale for both 800G (current volume) and ramping 1.6T. They demonstrated a 3.2T prototype at OFC 2026 with Ciena, putting them at the frontier of the next generation.

**Key moat:** Volume scale in transceiver manufacturing, NVIDIA strategic partnership, 3.2T roadmap, silicon photonics + InP + GaAs multi-material capability.

**Key risks:** Hyperscaler capex slowdown, silicon photonics commoditization from Broadcom/Intel, premium valuation, integration complexity from legacy II-VI merger.
