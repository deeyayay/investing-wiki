## One-Line Thesis
The picks-and-shovels play on the AI optical networking buildout — test & measurement + thin-film optical coatings uniquely positioned as AI fabric speeds race toward 1.6T Ethernet and silicon photonics scale-up.

## Investment Thesis
Viavi Solutions (formerly JDS Uniphase) operates at the intersection of two powerful AI infrastructure tailwinds: validating increasingly complex AI fabric interconnects, and scaling silicon photonics manufacturing. NSE segment provides test tooling that hyperscalers, telcos, and NEMs use to validate high-speed optical networks — at OFC 2026 they demoed 1.6T Ethernet, PCIe over optics, and silicon photonic manufacturing test. OSP segment produces high-volume thin-film optical coatings for anti-counterfeiting, 3D sensing, automotive, and aerospace. Founded 1923 (formerly JDS Uniphase).

**Key moat:** Deep domain expertise in optical test/measurement, 100+ years of optical coating IP, sticky OEM and carrier relationships, multi-segment defensibility (NSE + OSP).

**Key risks:** Revenue lumpiness tied to telco/hyperscaler capex cycles, OSP segment can be volatile, competition from Spirent and Keysight in test, stretched valuation (~177% premium to fair value per Morningstar).
