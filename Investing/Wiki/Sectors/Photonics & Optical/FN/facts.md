## Company Overview

Fabrinet is the world's premier contract manufacturer for optical and photonic components — the TSMC of the photonics industry. Major photonics OEMs (Coherent, Lumentum, Ciena, MACOM) outsource precision optical assembly to Fabrinet because optical alignment, fiber bonding, and hermetic sealing require specialized cleanroom expertise at scale that no individual OEM can operate as efficiently. Fabrinet's Thailand facilities (Pinehurst and Chonburi campuses) are NVIDIA-certified for AI transceiver assemblies, directly benefiting from the AI cluster buildout. Operating margins of ~15% are extraordinary for contract manufacturing — reflecting pricing power from unique process knowledge.
