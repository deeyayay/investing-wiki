## Company Overview

ShunSin Technology is a Taiwan-listed ODM manufacturer of fiber optic components and optical communication modules. The company serves the data center and telecom markets with SFP/SFP+/QSFP transceiver modules, PON (passive optical network) components, and fiber optic assemblies — primarily as a contract or ODM manufacturer for downstream system integrators and telecom operators. Taiwan's dense fiber optic manufacturing ecosystem gives ShunSin competitive manufacturing costs, but the market is extremely competitive with no proprietary technology moat distinguishing it from dozens of similar Taiwan and China-based ODMs.

## Key Metrics

- Market cap: ~$100M USD (very small; TWSE listed)
- Revenue: ~$45M USD (~NT$1.4B)
- Products: SFP/QSFP transceivers, PON components, fiber optic assemblies (ODM)
- Market: commodity optical components; Taiwan ODM ecosystem
- TWSE-listed; no US ADR; very limited coverage
