# LITE — Lumentum Holdings
*Last updated: May 11, 2026*

---

## One-Line Thesis
The specialized performance leader in AI optical components — sole supplier of 200G-per-lane EMLs at volume, $400M+ optical circuit switch backlog sold out through 2027, and a $2B NVIDIA partnership anchoring a breakout year for laser chip sales.

## Investment Thesis

> **Thesis established:** 2026-05-11
> **Last validated:** 2026-05-12
> **Drift status:** On track — sole 200G EML supplier at volume; $400M+ OCS backlog sold out through 2027; no viable second source yet

Lumentum is the most technically differentiated name in the photonics stack. They are currently the only supplier shipping 200G-per-lane electro-absorption modulated lasers (EMLs) at volume — the critical component for next-generation 1.6T pluggable transceivers. NVIDIA committed $2B to Lumentum in March 2026 alongside multi-year, multi-billion-dollar purchase commitments. Q2 FY2026 revenue: $665.5M (+65.5% YoY), non-GAAP gross margin 42.5%, operating margin 25.2%. Optical circuit switch backlog exceeds $400M and is sold out through 2027. Management targets a $1.25B quarterly run rate within 9-12 months and a $2B run rate within 2 years. Added to the S&P 500 in March 2026.

**Key moat:** Sole 200G EML supplier at volume, optical circuit switch leadership, NVIDIA anchor customer, S&P 500 inclusion driving index demand.

**Key risks:** Premium valuation (60x+), hyperscaler capex cycle dependence, eventual EML second-source risk, manufacturing capacity constraints could flip to oversupply.

**Execution track record:** Hurlston stepped into Lumentum during a pivotal inflection — FY2025 delivered 21% revenue growth to $1.65B, 450 bps gross margin expansion, and non-GAAP EPS more than quadrupled under his watch. He previously oversaw Finisar's sale to II-VI at a premium, and turned around Synaptics' product mix toward higher-margin enterprise IoT. Ali has maintained financial discipline across multiple cycles and has been a stabilizing CFO presence since 2019. The prior CEO (Alan Lowe, ~10 years) spun the company out of JDS Uniphase and built the foundational optical networking franchise now scaling into AI.

**Insider ownership / alignment:** DEF 14A filed Oct 7, 2025 notes significant share ownership guidelines for all executive officers and directors. Recent insider activity shows 29 open-market transactions in the prior 6 months, all sales — no purchases. Hurlston's compensation package ($27.7M total, ~99% equity-linked) provides strong upside alignment. No meaningful open-market buying from current insiders on record.
