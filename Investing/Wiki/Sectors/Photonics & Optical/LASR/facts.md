## Company Overview

nLIGHT designs and manufactures high-power semiconductor and fiber lasers for industrial and defense applications. The company competes in two very different markets: (1) Industrial fiber lasers for metal cutting, welding, and additive manufacturing — a market dominated by IPG Photonics, which holds structural pricing and scale advantages; (2) Defense/government lasers for directed energy weapons (DEW), missile defense systems, and military sensing — a market where nLIGHT's US manufacturing, DoD security clearances, and specialized high-power laser expertise create a differentiated position. The defense segment carries higher margins and has benefited from growing DoD investment in laser weapon systems (counter-drone, missile defense) through programs with Northrop Grumman, Raytheon, and L3Harris.
