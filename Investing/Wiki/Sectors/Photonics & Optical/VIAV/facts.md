## Company Overview

Viavi Solutions is the test and measurement arm of the former JDS Uniphase empire (split into Lumentum + Viavi in 2015). The company provides two types of value: (1) fiber optic test equipment (OTDR, optical power meters) used by telecom carriers to verify fiber network integrity — a critical quality gate before any fiber can carry live traffic; (2) optical security films for anti-counterfeiting on currency, pharmaceuticals, and government documents. The NSE division has high switching costs because carrier field technicians are trained on Viavi equipment and test workflows are embedded in operational procedures. The OSP division is a stable, recurring revenue stream with optical film contracts from central banks and government agencies.
