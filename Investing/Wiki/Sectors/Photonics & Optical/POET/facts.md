## Company Overview

POET Technologies is developing a monolithic optical interposer platform that integrates III-V photonic devices (lasers, modulators, photodetectors) with passive waveguides and electronics on a single chip. Traditional optical transceivers require hybrid bonding or wire bonding between separate chips — a process that introduces optical loss and increases manufacturing complexity. The POET Platform enables a fully integrated photonic circuit fabricated in a single process, potentially delivering lower cost and better performance at scale. Pre-revenue with commercial qualification underway at InnoLight (a major transceiver ODM supplier to hyperscalers).
