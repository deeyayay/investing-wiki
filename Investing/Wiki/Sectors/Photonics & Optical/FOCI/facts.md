## Company Overview

FOCI Fiber Optic Communications is a Taiwan-listed manufacturer of passive fiber optic components — the commodity end of the photonics supply chain. Products include fiber patch cords, adapters, connectors, splitters, and MPO/MTP high-density fiber interconnects used in data centers and telecom networks. This is an extremely competitive market with no proprietary technology moat; manufacturers compete on certification (UL, RoHS, Telcordia), quality consistency, and price. The tailwind is data center fiber demand (every AI server rack requires substantial fiber patch cord infrastructure) and telecom fiber buildout, but the benefit is shared across hundreds of manufacturers.
