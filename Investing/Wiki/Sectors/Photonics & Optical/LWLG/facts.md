## Company Overview

Lightwave Logic is a pre-revenue photonics materials company with a single mission: commercialize its Perkinite electro-optic polymer (EOP) platform as a replacement for lithium niobate (LiNbO3) modulators in silicon photonics. The physics is compelling — Perkinite achieves electro-optic coefficients exceeding 200 pm/V, roughly 6-7x higher than LiNbO3, enabling optical modulators that operate at <0.5V drive voltage vs. 5-6V for LiNbO3. In co-packaged optics (CPO) applications — the next-generation architecture connecting AI chip clusters — lower modulator drive voltage means dramatically lower power consumption per transceiver. If Perkinite can be manufactured reliably at silicon photonics foundry processes (CMOS-compatible deposition), it could become the dominant modulator material for the CPO era. The risk is all execution: materials science breakthrough → foundry compatibility → reliability qualification → customer adoption.
