## Company Overview

Anritsu is a 130-year-old Japanese precision instruments company with leadership positions in wireless communications test equipment and optical fiber test. The 5G test segment is the most relevant growth driver: as carriers globally deploy 5G networks and device manufacturers (Qualcomm, MediaTek, Apple, Samsung) require protocol conformance certification, Anritsu's ME7834NR callbox system is one of only a few platforms qualified for 3GPP-certified 5G testing. The optical OTDR business competes directly with Viavi in fiber network commissioning. The food/pharma inspection equipment business provides stable, non-cyclical revenue. Anritsu's gross margins (~50%) are strong for a hardware manufacturer, reflecting the T&M pricing power of specialized instruments.
