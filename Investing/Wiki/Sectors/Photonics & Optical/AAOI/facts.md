## Company Overview

Applied Optoelectronics is a vertically integrated optical transceiver company that designs and manufactures its own III-V semiconductor epitaxial layers through finished transceivers — one of the few companies globally with complete in-house supply chain for optical components. Founded in 1997 by Thompson Lin, the company serves CATV (cable network) and data center markets. AAOI gained and lost Wall Street attention dramatically in 2017 when Amazon (its largest customer, ~40% of revenue) reduced orders, collapsing the stock 90%. The AI buildout has revived data center transceiver demand, but AAOI lacks the dominant market position or brand recognition of Coherent, Lumentum, or InnoLight — limiting pricing power and investor confidence in sustained growth.

- Market cap: ~$1.5B

- Vertical integration: epitaxial wafer growth → chip fabrication → module assembly (in Sugar Land, TX + Taiwan)
