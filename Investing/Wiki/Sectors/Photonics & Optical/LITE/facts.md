## Company Overview

Lumentum is the photonics company most concentrated on high-value, IP-intensive components: VCSEL arrays (dominant Apple supplier for Face ID / LiDAR), ROADMs for telecom optical networks, and coherent optical transceivers. Spun out of JDSU in 2015, the company focused on premium components rather than commodity modules, achieving gross margins of 45–50%. The NeoPhotonics acquisition (2022) added InP-based coherent transceiver chips, extending capability into 400G/800G data center interconnect. The primary risk is Apple VCSEL concentration and telecom capex cyclicality.

- Market cap: ~$3B
- Revenue FY2025: ~$1.4B

- Apple VCSEL: #1 supplier for iPhone Face ID + LiDAR (structural product anchor)
- ROADM: #2 globally; recovering as telecom capex normalizes
- 800ZR coherent transceivers: ramping for data center interconnect
- NeoPhotonics InP: added coherent chip vertical integration (2022 acquisition)
