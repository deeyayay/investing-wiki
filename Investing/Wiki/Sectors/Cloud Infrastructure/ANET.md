*Last updated: May 7, 2026*

## One-Line Thesis
The dominant Ethernet switching platform for hyperscaler AI clusters; as AI workloads shift from InfiniBand to Ethernet, Arista is the primary beneficiary with proven software-driven networking at scale.

---

## Investment Thesis
Arista sells high-speed Ethernet switches and its EOS (Extensible Operating System) software to the world's largest data centers. The AI angle is this: NVIDIA GPU clusters originally ran on InfiniBand (NVDA's Quantum networking), but hyperscalers at scale are increasingly moving to Ethernet because it's cheaper, more flexible, and their engineering teams already know it. Arista dominates high-speed Ethernet switching (400G/800G) and has deep relationships with all five hyperscalers. As AI cluster sizes balloon to hundreds of thousands of GPUs, the networking layer becomes as important as the compute layer. Arista's software moat (EOS) means customers don't just buy switches — they build their entire network automation stack around Arista's APIs. Founder Jay Frisch and CEO Jayshree Ullal have run this company with exceptional discipline.

**Key moat:** EOS software ecosystem lock-in, hyperscaler relationships (Meta, Microsoft, Google are top customers), 400G/800G switching leadership, founder-led culture.

**Key risks:** Cisco competitive response, InfiniBand resurgence (NVDA Quantum-X), customer concentration (Meta ~15% of revenue), custom silicon from hyperscalers.
