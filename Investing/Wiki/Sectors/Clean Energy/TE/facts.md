## Company Overview

T1 Energy is a Texas-focused utility-scale solar and battery energy storage developer. The company specializes in ERCOT (Texas grid) project development, leveraging IRA Investment Tax Credits to improve project economics. The Texas grid crisis accelerated corporate demand for on-site or dedicated renewable power — T1 Energy's customer base is data centers, industrial facilities, and utilities seeking long-term clean power purchase agreements. Project-based revenue creates lumpy quarterly results and capital intensity limits scalability without ongoing project finance access.

## Key Metrics

- Market cap: ~$500M
- Revenue FY2025: ~$180M (project-based, lumpy)
- Pipeline: ~2.5 GW solar + storage under development in Texas
- Gross margin: ~28% (EPC/developer margins under compression)
- IRA ITC: 30-40% investment tax credit improving project IRRs
- Pre-profitability; capital-intensive project finance model
