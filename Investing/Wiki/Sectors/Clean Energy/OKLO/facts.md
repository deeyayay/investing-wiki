## Company Overview

Oklo is developing the Aurora micro-fission reactor — a compact, self-contained nuclear power plant producing 1.5-50 MWe that can be deployed at data center sites, military installations, and industrial facilities. The key differentiator vs. other SMR developers is the metal fuel cycle: Aurora runs on High-Assay Low-Enriched Uranium (HALEU) and can recycle spent nuclear fuel — closing the fuel cycle and addressing the nuclear waste problem simultaneously. The company went public via Sam Altman's AltaVista SPAC in 2024. The NRC rejected the initial Aurora license application in 2022 for insufficient safety documentation; a revised combined license application is under review.
