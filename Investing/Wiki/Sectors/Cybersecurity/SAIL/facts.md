## Company Overview

SailPoint is the enterprise identity governance platform — the system that decides who (and what) gets access to which corporate resources, and audits that access continuously. Embedded in ~50% of Fortune 500 companies with 150+ deep enterprise connectors, including AWS to legacy COBOL mainframes. As machine identities (AI agents, bots, APIs, service accounts) multiply to 45:1 vs. humans by 2026, every machine identity needs SailPoint's provisioning, governance, and audit capabilities. Re-IPO'd in 2024 following Thoma Bravo private ownership (2022–2024).
