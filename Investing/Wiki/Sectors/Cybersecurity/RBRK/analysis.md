## One-Line Thesis

Rubrik is the cloud-native data security platform that ransomware makes mandatory — 46% ARR growth and 120% NRR at $1.46B ARR with founder-CEO Bipul Sinha holding 8.9%.

## Investment Thesis

Ransomware is the single most reliable demand driver in enterprise security: every successful attack at a peer company is a Rubrik sales event. The platform's zero-trust architecture replaces legacy backup vendors (Veeam, Commvault) with immutable, air-gapped cloud-native storage that cannot be encrypted by ransomware. Once installed, Rubrik becomes the last-resort recovery layer — the highest-switching-cost position in the security stack.

**Why now:** Cloud migrations are accelerating the rip-and-replace cycle from legacy backup. Machine identities (AI agents, bots, service accounts) are multiplying attack surfaces faster than human administrators can manage. Rubrik's data classification and access governance layer is expanding from backup/recovery into broader data security posture management (DSPM), expanding TAM from ~$10B to $40B+.

**Moat:** Cloud-native architecture (zero-trust by design), deep enterprise integrations across 1,000+ enterprise customers, 120% NRR proving land-and-expand, and a government-credentialed security team (former CIA CISO Michael Mestrovich, ex-Palo Alto CEO Mark McLaughlin on board).

**Risk:** Pre-profitability (-35% operating margin) requires sustained growth to justify $18B market cap. Crowded enterprise security market (CrowdStrike, Palo Alto, Cohesity). Founder Bipul Sinha running 10b5-1 sales plan.
