## Company Overview

Rubrik is the cloud-native data security and resilience platform. As ransomware attacks proliferate and cloud migrations accelerate, Rubrik's zero-trust data security architecture becomes mandatory enterprise infrastructure. The platform is deeply embedded in enterprise IT (high switching costs), replacing legacy backup vendors (Veeam, Commvault) with modern cloud-native architecture. Founder Bipul Sinha holds ~8.9% ownership.
