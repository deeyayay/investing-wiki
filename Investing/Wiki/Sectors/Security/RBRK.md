# RBRK — Rubrik Inc.
*Last updated: May 7, 2026*

---

## One-Line Thesis
Cybersecurity data resilience platform with sticky enterprise SaaS revenue; beneficiary of ransomware tailwinds and cloud migration; founder-led with strong ARR growth trajectory.

---

## Investment Thesis
Rubrik has carved out a defensible position in data security and backup — a category that becomes more critical every year as ransomware attacks proliferate. The product is deeply embedded in enterprise IT infrastructure (high switching costs), and the transition from legacy backup vendors (Veeam, Commvault) is still early. The SaaS subscription model means ARR compounds predictably. Founder-led with Bipul Sinha still at the helm. The thesis is that data protection becomes a board-level priority, not an IT afterthought, and Rubrik is the category leader in modern cloud-native environments.

**Key moat:** Deep enterprise integrations, cloud-native architecture, zero-trust data security positioning, high NRR.

**Key risks:** Path to profitability still long, competitive pressure from Cohesity and Veeam, enterprise IT spending sensitivity to macro, valuation premium for pre-profit SaaS.
