## Company Overview

Super Micro Computer builds GPU servers that go into AI data centers — DGX-compatible rack systems, NVL72 GPU rack solutions, and high-density compute nodes. NVIDIA's closest hardware partner with first-mover advantage in liquid-cooled AI server design. High revenue leverage to AI buildout but significantly discounted by governance overhang from 2024 accounting delays, auditor resignations, and DOJ investigation. Filings are now current.
