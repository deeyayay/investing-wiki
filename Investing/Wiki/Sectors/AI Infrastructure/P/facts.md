## Company Overview

Everpure (fka Pure Storage) is the all-flash storage platform purpose-built for AI-era data gravity. The core innovation is DirectFlash — bypassing commodity SSD controllers to speak directly to NAND at the software layer, enabling 4-6x the density and performance of legacy NVMe SSD arrays. The Evergreen subscription model (no fork-lift upgrades, hardware refreshes included in subscription) creates recurring revenue with 90%+ renewal rates. Portworx (acquired 2020) extends the platform into Kubernetes-native persistent storage for AI pipelines, training data lakes, and inference serving.
