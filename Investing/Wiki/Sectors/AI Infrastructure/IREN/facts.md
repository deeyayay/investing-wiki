## Company Overview

Iris Energy is a vertically integrated AI data center operator controlling power, land, and cooling directly — eliminating landlord markup and cloud intermediary layers. Operates three segments: AI Cloud Services (managed GPU clusters), AI Data Centers (colocation/build-to-suit), and Bitcoin Mining (dynamic capacity buffer reallocated to GPU workloads based on ROI). NVIDIA designates IREN's Sweetwater Campus (Texas, 2GW) as the flagship DSX architecture deployment.
