## Company Overview

Vertiv makes the critical infrastructure that keeps AI data centers running: power distribution, UPS systems, thermal management (air and liquid cooling), and monitoring software. Chip-agnostic — they win regardless of whether the GPU cluster runs NVIDIA, AMD, or custom silicon. Every NVL72 Blackwell rack requires liquid cooling that only Vertiv-scale providers can deliver at hyperscale.
