# Vertiv Holdings — Investment Analysis

## One-Line Thesis

The picks-and-shovels play on AI data center thermal management and power delivery — chip-agnostic, $15B+ backlog, and every GPU cluster needs Vertiv infrastructure regardless of whose chips are inside.

## Investment Thesis

| Date | Status | Notes |

| 2026-06-13 | Scored | 8.0/10 Unrivaled |

Vertiv makes the critical infrastructure that keeps AI data centers running: power distribution, UPS systems, thermal management (air and liquid cooling), and monitoring software. The AI era has dramatically increased demand because GPU clusters (especially NVL72 Blackwell racks) generate heat densities that legacy air cooling cannot handle — liquid cooling is now mandatory at scale.

The most important fact: Vertiv is **chip-agnostic**. They get the order whether the data center runs NVIDIA, AMD, or custom silicon. This removes the binary chip competition risk entirely. Combined with a $15B+ backlog (more than double the prior year) with Q4 2025 orders up 152% YoY, visibility into future revenue is exceptional.

The company benefits from a massive replacement cycle: long-installed-base data center customers are upgrading to AI-ready infrastructure. This wave compounds on itself because each new AI deployment also requires Vertiv power and cooling for the entire rack, not just the GPU layer.

**Key moat:** Chip-agnostic installed base + service contract recurring revenue + liquid cooling IP + global service network + long-term supply agreements.

**Key risks:** Supply chain constraints on liquid cooling components; execution risk on $15B backlog conversion; competition from Schneider Electric and Eaton; new CFO integration risk.
