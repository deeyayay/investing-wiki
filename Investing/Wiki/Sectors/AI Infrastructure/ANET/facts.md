## Company Overview

Arista Networks is the dominant Ethernet switching platform for hyperscaler AI clusters. Its EOS (Extensible Operating System) software creates deep ecosystem lock-in — hyperscalers build their entire network automation stack around Arista APIs. As AI clusters shift from InfiniBand to Ethernet, Arista is the primary network beneficiary across Meta, Microsoft, and Google.
