## Summary
Lightwave Logic remains an essentially pre-revenue development-stage company as of Q3 FY2025 with ~$29K in quarterly revenue. Cash of $34.9M as of September 30, 2025 is the lifeline — at a ~$5-6M quarterly burn rate, the company has roughly 5-7 quarters of runway from this date (through mid-2027), aligning with the 2027 production ramp target. The company underwent a significant leadership transition in December 2024 with Yves LeMaitre (optical communications industry veteran) installed as CEO to drive commercialization — a positive signal. Shares have been increasing through equity issuances (123M → 132M in 9 months), creating dilution risk. The thesis is entirely dependent on a production commitment from one of the four Stage 3 design-win customers before cash runs out.

## Cash Runway Analysis ⚠️
- Cash: $34.9M at Sep 30, 2025
- Quarterly burn: ~$5-6M
- Runway estimate: ~6-7 quarters from Sep 30, 2025 → **through approximately Q1-Q2 2027**
- This runway barely covers the 2027 production ramp target — **if production ramp slips, LWLG faces a capital raise**
- Equity dilution already underway: +9.4M shares in 9 months (123M → 132.7M, +7.6%)

**Note:** The "$70M cash" figure cited in some analysis may reflect expected future capital raises or be from a different reporting date. Actual as-filed is $34.9M at Sep 30, 2025.
