## Summary
AAOI delivered a strong FY2025 with revenue of $455.7M (+82.7% YoY) and gross margin improvement to 30.0% from 24.8%, narrowing the net loss to -$38.2M from -$186.7M. The internet data center segment (42.9% of revenue) is the growth engine driven by hyperscaler 800G+ demand. However, a critical risk is disclosed: **a single customer, Digicomm, accounted for 53.1% of FY2025 revenue** — an extreme concentration that materially de-risks the thesis if Digicomm order patterns shift. Microsoft (data center) is the second largest customer at 28.8%.

## Customer Concentration ⚠️ CRITICAL RISK
| Customer | % FY2025 Revenue | Segment |
|----------|-----------------|---------|
| **Digicomm** | **53.1%** | **CATV** |
| **Microsoft** | **28.8%** | **Data Center** |
| Top 2 customers | **81.9%** | — |

- Digicomm was only 34.1% in 2024 and 11.3% in 2023 — this concentration is accelerating rapidly
- Any pullback from Digicomm would catastrophically impact revenue
- The data center thesis (Microsoft + 800G+) is strong but only 42.9% of revenue currently

## Business Highlights
- **Vertically integrated:** Designs and manufactures own laser chips using proprietary MBE and MOCVD processes — rare capability outside of LITE/COHR
- **US manufacturing in Sugar Land, TX:** Competitive advantage given supply chain localization trends; domestic laser manufacturing for data center products
- **800G ramp:** In-house epitaxial growth + highly automated manufacturing = ability to scale rapidly; actively ramping 800G and above
- **Quantum Bandwidth™ brand:** Direct-to-MSO product line launched 2023 — reducing intermediary margin leak in CATV

---

## Key Risks
1. **Extreme customer concentration** — Digicomm 53.1%, Microsoft 28.8% = 81.9% top-2 concentration
2. **Continued net losses** — profitable at gross level but net losses persist; accumulated deficit $493.1M
3. **CATV vs. DC mix** — 53.8% CATV is less exciting than the DC narrative suggests
4. **No moat vs. incumbents** — competing against COHR/LITE/FN in data center with smaller scale
