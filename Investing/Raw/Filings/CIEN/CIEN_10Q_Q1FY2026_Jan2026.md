## Summary
Ciena's Q1 FY2026 was a landmark quarter with revenue of $1.43B (+33.1% YoY) and net income of $150.3M (+237% YoY, $1.03 diluted EPS). This is the strongest quarter in Ciena's history by revenue. Gross margin held steady at 43.8% on dramatically higher revenue, demonstrating pricing power and operating leverage. Deferred revenue surged to $390.9M (current + LT), signaling an exceptionally strong backlog and forward demand visibility. The balance sheet is solid: $1.3B in cash/investments, manageable $1.5B LT debt, and $878M in deferred tax assets. R&D investment at $221M in a single quarter confirms Ciena is accelerating toward the 3.2T/next-gen roadmap.
