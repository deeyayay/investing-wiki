## Summary
Fabrinet's Q3 FY2026 (December 27, 2025 – March 27, 2026) continued the sector's breakout trajectory with revenue of $1.21B (+39.3% YoY), net income of $125.2M (+54.0%), and diluted EPS of $3.45. The 9-month revenue run rate of $3.33B puts Fabrinet on track for a ~$4.4B fiscal year. Inventory build of +50.8% signals deep order book loading for the 1.6T ramp cycle. The balance sheet remains exceptionally clean — no long-term debt, $945M in cash and short-term investments, $2.43B in retained earnings. A new HPC (High Performance Computing) revenue category appeared this period, signaling AI cluster product diversification beyond DCI.
