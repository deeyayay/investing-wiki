## Summary
VIAV's Q3 FY2026 (December 28, 2025 – March 28, 2026) showed dramatic revenue acceleration (+42.8% YoY to $406.8M), driven heavily by an undisclosed acquisition that added substantial goodwill (+$106M) and intangibles (+$266M) to the balance sheet this fiscal year. Net income was suppressed at just $6.4M ($0.03 diluted EPS) due to $17.3M in restructuring charges and $3.7M debt extinguishment losses — noise from the acquisition integration. On a 9-month basis, the $46.2M loss on debt extinguishment has masked otherwise strong operating results. Core organic trends are positive: product revenue nearly doubled in Q3 to $357M vs $241.5M prior year.
