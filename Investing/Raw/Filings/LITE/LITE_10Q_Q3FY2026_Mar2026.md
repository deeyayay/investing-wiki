## Summary
Lumentum's Q3 FY2026 (December 28, 2025 – March 28, 2026) was a landmark quarter: revenue of $808.4M nearly doubled YoY (+90.1%) and the company posted $144.2M in net income vs. a $44.1M loss in the prior year period. The $2B NVIDIA Series A Preferred Stock investment hit the balance sheet this quarter, vaulting cash to $2.62B. The 2026 convertible notes maturity (~$3.24B current debt) is being managed through a combination of new 2032 Notes issuance ($1.25B), the NVIDIA preferred proceeds ($2B), and partial repurchases. CapEx running at $284.5M in 9 months (+60.6% YoY) signals continued aggressive capacity build-out for the CPO and 1.6T ramp.

## MD&A Key Points
- Revenue acceleration driven entirely by Components segment (laser chips, EML, optical circuit switches) — the AI optical buildout is the driver
- Gross margin expansion: Q3 FY2026 gross profit $357M (44.2% margin) vs $122.5M (28.8%) prior year — ~1,540 bps improvement
- SG&A declining as % of revenue ($90.8M on $808M revenue = 11.2% vs $112M on $425M = 26.4%) — operating leverage kicking in
- Restructuring charges collapsing: $9M in 9 months vs $17.6M — operational cleanup nearly complete
- CapEx: $284.5M in 9 months vs $177.1M — capacity expansion accelerating

---

## Key Developments
- **NVIDIA $2B Series A Convertible Preferred Stock:** Received $1,999.7M in proceeds this period — landmark strategic commitment formally on the balance sheet. 2.9M preferred shares outstanding as of March 28, 2026
- **2032 Convertible Notes:** Issued $1,254.7M in new 2032 Notes (net of issuance costs) to refinance the 2026 Notes — debt maturity wall being proactively managed
- **2026 Notes partial repurchase:** $843.1M repurchased; ~$3.24B still showing as current — watch Q4 FY2026 for full resolution
- **Acquisition:** Paid $38M for a business acquisition in the 9-month period (undisclosed)
- **Capped Call Options:** Paid $102M for capped calls on the 2032 Notes — limits dilution on conversion

---

## Debt Maturity Watch ⚠️
- $3.24B in current portion of LT debt as of March 28, 2026 — represents 2026 Notes maturing in the near term
- Mitigation: $2B NVIDIA preferred + $1.25B 2032 Notes + operating cash flow ($388M in 9 months) provides coverage
- Key risk: If any refinancing plans are disrupted, this becomes a material balance sheet risk
