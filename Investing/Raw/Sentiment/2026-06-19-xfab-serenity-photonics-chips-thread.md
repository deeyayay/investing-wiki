## Overall Signal Assessment

**Signal strength:** HIGH — multiple independently verifiable primary claims. The author has done original research (EDGAR filings, PCN documents, EU project databases).

**Thesis impact:** SIGNIFICANT UPGRADE for XFAB wiki. The photonixFAB silicon photonics angle (NVIDIA + Nokia consortium + EU government backing) is a major dimension entirely absent from our initial KB entry. This is not a generic "maybe photonics will matter" thesis — NVIDIA was at the 5th consortium meeting in June 2025.

**Risks to the thesis:**
1. Evaluation → contract gap: This is the critical open question. The market will not re-rate XFAB until NVIDIA/Nokia announce volume commitments, and that is H2 2027 at earliest per the author's own timeline.
2. Yield risk: MTP (micro-transfer printing) at wafer scale is still developmental — "yields still behind" per the author. This is honest and key.
3. EU grant conflation: The €128M is for MEMS (different from photonics). Investors combining these may be double-counting.
4. NVTS GaN diversification means XFAB→NVTS SiC is not as clean as presented.
