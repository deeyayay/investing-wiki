What if I told you there was a more discreet way to bet on the future of humanity?
Today, the most popular narratives are quite clear: space, robotics, humanoid robots, AI infrastructure, data centers, electrification.
But behind all these themes lies a much less sexy, yet absolutely indispensable building block: microcontrollers / MCUs.
An MCU is essentially the "little brain" embedded inside a machine.
It allows for the control, measurement, automation, and securing of specific functions: motors, sensors, power supply, temperature, connectivity, energy management, embedded systems, etc.
In short: it is the common denominator of all these narratives.
The most obvious exposures to play this theme are:

A highly diversified European player exposed to MCUs, automotive, industry, sensors, analog, and power semiconductors. My top pick, with contracts including SpaceX and AWS, among others.
