Breakdown of TSMC's CoPoS Equipment List

1. Lithography and Coating
TSMC has introduced a highly comprehensive lineup for exposure and coating equipment:
· Canon (Japan): FPA-5525iV LF2 exposure equipment
· SUSS MicroTec (Germany): DSC310s Gen4 exposure machines and ACS310 Gen2 coat/develop cluster platforms
· Tokyo Electron (Japan): LITHIUS Pro SQ3
· SCREEN (Japan): LM-3000
· Scientech (Taiwan): Panel-level release layer coating equipment

2. Metallization and Copper Plating
CoPoS requires larger RDLs and finer line processes, so equipment requirements have been upgraded:
· Applied Materials (US), KLA (US), & Leading Precision (Taiwan): Secured spots on the list
· Lam Research (US): Providing SABRE 3D FP for copper plating and the Quaros FP for UBM etching

3. Grinding, Laser Processing, & Die Bonding
· DISCO (Japan): Swept orders for grinding & related laser processing tools
· Nitto Denko (Japan): Frame Mount and UV Erasing equipment
· LINTEC (Japan): Lamination and De-taping processes
· Kulicke & Soffa (Singapore): APTURA WP for fluidless die attach
· ASMPT (Hong Kong): Firebird XQ for fluidless die attach
· Shibaura (Japan): Providing TFC-6600-WB and TFC-6500-WB systems
· All Ring (Taiwan): Supplying underfill equipment

4. Wet Processes & Thermal Treatment
· Grand Process Technology (GPTC) & Scientech (both Taiwan): for wet process equipment
· AblePrint (Taiwan): BPO-60A baking equipment
· Kokusai Electric (Japan): 450A and 450A-HT equipment
· Csun Mfg. (Taiwan): HOMOL-AP31, HP-AP31, and CSL-A300PL thermal treatment systems

5. Molding & Reflow
· TOWA, APIC YAMADA (Japan): Molding equipment
· SEMIgear, Heller (US): Reflow ovens

6. Metrology and Inspection
With the introduction of glass substrates to CoPoS, the importance of metrology and inspection has skyrocketed, allowing Taiwanese firms to capture critical positions:
· V5 Tech (Taiwan): V5P310 Pro Glass AXI inspection equipment alongside the V5300 Macro AOI system
· Favite (Taiwan): Handling overlay metrology
· Weike Semi (Taiwan): 3D profiling and dimensional measurement
· Mirle Automation (Taiwan): Partnering with Japan's KOBELCO to supply bevel inspection and bonding shift measurement tools.

Other Taiwanese manufacturers making the list include Chroma ATE, Gudeng Precision, Gallant Micro (GMM), Ta Liang, YaYa Tech, Nivek, and Semtek Corp.
